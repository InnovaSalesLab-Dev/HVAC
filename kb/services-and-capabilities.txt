# services-and-capabilities.md
**Lookup-only reference for what Valley View HVAC does, doesn’t do, and typical visit types.**

---

## Service types & capabilities
### Residential
- Whole home ducted systems
- Split home ducted systems
- Ductless systems

### Commercial
- Wall-hung packaged units
- Roof-mounted packaged units
- Air-controlled systems

---

## Not serviced
Valley View HVAC **does not** service:
- Radiant systems
- Geothermal systems
- Hydro/steam systems
- Boiler services

### Related work (special handling)
- Valley View HVAC **can** fit ducted/ductless systems and **abandon/sub out boiler removal** (case-by-case)

---

## Appointment types & typical durations
> These are typical ranges; actual duration depends on scope and findings.

### Diagnostic
- On-site diagnostic: **20–30 minutes** (scheduled as **1 hour** block)

### Proposal / estimate
- **20–50 minutes** (varies by project scope)

### Repair
- **15 minutes to 6.5+ hours** (post-diagnosis)

### Installation
- **2.5–4 hours** (simple)  
- **2–3 full days** (complex)
