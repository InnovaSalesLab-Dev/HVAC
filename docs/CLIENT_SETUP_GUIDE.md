## üéØ How It All Works Together

### **The Complete Flow:**

1. **Customer sees your ad** (Google or Facebook)
2. **Clicks ad** ‚Üí Goes to your website
3. **Fills out contact form** on your website
4. **Form submits** ‚Üí Creates contact in GoHighLevel
5. **Our system automatically calls them** within seconds
6. **If call fails** ‚Üí Sends SMS fallback message

**OR**

1. **Customer clicks Facebook Lead Ad** ‚Üí Fills form directly in Facebook
2. **Facebook sends lead to GoHighLevel** ‚Üí Creates contact
3. **Our system automatically calls them**

## üìù Part 1: Create the Form in GoHighLevel

### **Step 1: Create Contact Form**

1. Go to **Sites ‚Üí Forms** in GoHighLevel
2. Click **"Create Form"**
3. Name it: **"Contact Form"** or **"Service Request Form"**
4. Add fields:
   - **First Name** (required)
   - **Last Name** (required)
   - **Phone Number** (required)
   - **Email** (optional but recommended)
   - **Address** (optional)
   - **Message/Service Needed** (optional text area)
5. Click **"Save"**

### **Step 2: Configure Form Settings**

1. Click on your form ‚Üí **"Options"** tab
2. **On Submit Actions:**
   - ‚úÖ **"Add to Contacts"** (MUST be enabled)
   - ‚úÖ **"Add Tag: website"** (to mark as website lead)
   - ‚úÖ **"Add to Workflow"** (optional - if you have follow-up workflows)
3. **Form Settings:**
   - Enable **"Show on Mobile"**
   - Enable **"Show Thank You Message"**
   - Thank you message: *"Thank you! We'll call you shortly."*

### **Step 3: Get Form Embed Code**

1. Click **"Share"** or **"Embed"** button
2. Choose **"Embed Code"** (iframe)
3. Copy the entire embed code
4. **Save this code** - you'll need it for your website

## üåê Part 2: Embed Form on Your Website

### **Option A: Embed on Existing Website (Recommended)**

1. **Log into your website** (WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, etc.)
2. **Go to the page** where you want the form (usually "Contact" or "Get Quote")
3. **Add a new section** or edit existing content
4. **Paste the embed code** you copied from GoHighLevel
5. **Save and publish** the page
6. **Test it:** Fill out the form and submit - should create contact in GHL

### **Option B: Create Landing Page in GoHighLevel**

1. Go to **Sites ‚Üí Funnels** in GoHighLevel
2. Create new funnel/page
3. Add your form to the page
4. Publish the page
5. **Use this page URL** as your ad landing page

## üîµ Part 3: Facebook Ads Setup

### **Option A: Facebook Lead Ads (Recommended - No Website Needed)**

**What it is:** Customer fills form directly in Facebook, no website visit needed.

**Setup Steps:**

1. **Go to Facebook Ads Manager** ‚Üí Create new campaign
2. **Choose objective:** "Leads"
3. **Ad Set Settings:**
   - **Audience:** Target your service area (Salem, OR + 20-25 mile radius)
   - **Placements:** Facebook Feed, Instagram Feed
   - **Budget:** Set your daily budget
4. **Create Ad:**
   - **Ad Format:** Choose "Lead Ad"
   - **Headline:** "Valley View HVAC - Expert Heating & Cooling"
   - **Text:** "Need HVAC service? Fill out this quick form and we'll call you!"
   - **Image/Video:** Use your company logo or service photos
5. **Lead Form Setup:**
   - **Form Type:** "More Volume" (simpler form)
   - **Form Fields:** 
     - First Name (required)
     - Last Name (required)
     - Phone Number (required)
     - Email (optional)
   - **Privacy Policy:** Add link to your privacy policy
   - **Thank You Screen:** "Thank you! We'll call you shortly."
6. **Connect to GoHighLevel:**
   - In Facebook Lead Form settings, find **"CRM Integration"**
   - Choose **"GoHighLevel"** (if available)
   - OR use **Zapier:**
     - Create Zap: **Facebook Lead Ads ‚Üí GoHighLevel**
     - When new lead ‚Üí Create/Update Contact in GHL
     - Add tag: **"facebook"** to mark lead source
7. **Publish Ad**

**How it works:**
- Customer clicks ad ‚Üí Sees form in Facebook
- Fills form ‚Üí Facebook sends to GoHighLevel
- GoHighLevel creates contact with tag "facebook"
- Our system calls them automatically

## üö® Important Notes

1. **Only ONE workflow should be active:**
   - Use "Contact Created" workflow (not "Form Submitted")
   - This prevents duplicate calls

2. **Lead Source Tags:**
   - Website forms ‚Üí Tag: `website`
   - Facebook leads ‚Üí Tag: `facebook`
   - Google leads ‚Üí Tag: `google`
   - Yelp ‚Üí Tag: `yelp`
   - Thumbtack ‚Üí Tag: `thumbtack`

3. **Inbound Calls:**
   - People who call your number directly are marked as `lead_source = "inbound"`
   - They will NOT receive outbound calls or SMS fallback
   - They only get confirmation SMS after booking appointment

4. **Testing:**
   - Always test with fake phone numbers first
   - Check GoHighLevel Execution Logs to verify webhooks are firing
   - Check backend logs: `flyctl logs -a scott-valley-hvac-api`
